WEBSITE HELP – Deen Toolkit

🟢 Navigation:
- Click the ☰ (hamburger icon) to open sidebar
- Choose “Date Converter” or “ Divine & Prophetic Names”

🕓 How to Use:
1. Enter a Gregorian date in this format: DD/MM/YYYY or YYYY-MM-DD
2. Click Enter-Key ↵ to auto-convert
3. View Hijri dates and use copy buttons

💡 Tips:
- Click 🌙 icon on top-right to switch light/dark theme
- Tap any name (in Asma section) to copy Arabic, transliteration, or meaning

⚠️ Troubleshooting:
- Invalid date? Use only valid Gregorian ranges (1937–2080)
- Dark mode not switching? Clear localStorage or refresh page
- Best viewed in Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari
